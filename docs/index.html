About this Map

This map is based on data from the Town Hall Project 2018. I like working with maps and data, so I decided to give visualizing this important dataset a shot. All credit for the data collected goes to the people behind the Town Hall Project. I hope that this tool will help folks get involved with their members of congress more easily.

About the Town Hall Project

The core of the Town Hall Project is its spreadsheet.
Please send all questions/comments/updates about town hall data to townhallproject2018@gmail.com
Find the Town Hall Project on Twitter and Facebook

Data are compiled by volunteers from each Congress member's official website, Twitter, or Facebook.

Glossary

Town Hall
A forum where members of Congress give updates on the current affairs of Congress and answer questions from constituents
District Office Hours
Serves the same purpose as a Town Hall, however Elected Officials are not always expected to attend
Ticketed Event
Oftentimes county party events, local fundraisers, or campaign functions. There may be a fee for admission.
TeleTown Hall Meeting
A town hall conducted by conference call.
